# Source Policy

Store summaries, links, source ids, retrieval timestamps, license notes, review
flags, and content hashes. Do not store copied article bodies, long wiki page
extracts, or paywalled text.

Corpus notes are sourced context and hypotheses. They never replace OSRS Wiki
price truth, graph evidence, execution estimates, or recommendation logic.

Competitor capability notes are product-planning context only. Do not surface
them in recommendation copy, user alerts, or public-facing evidence trails.

Any corpus entry with unclear licensing, weak sourcing, or uncertain review
state should remain blocked from product logic until a human resolves it.
